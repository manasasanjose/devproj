Code of Conduct

The board of directors at this company has adopted the following Code of Conduct for its directors, officers and other employees. This code was resonably designed to deter the wrongdoings and to promote :


I. Honest and Ethical Conduct

Honest conduct is considered to be free from fraud and deception.Ethical conduct is dealing conflicts of interest between personal and professional ethically.

II. Ensure Financial Integrity

Ensure that the interal records are honored, financial records are accurate and correct annd ensure that the money is spent wisely. Never falsify any expenses for the company .

III. Obey the Law

Follow and obey the major rules and laws that apply to your work.

IV. Anti-Corruption

We do not tolerate corrupt behaviour including bribery or any kickbacks under any circumstances.

V. Respect for the Individual

We are an equal affirmative action employer and is committed to providing a workplace that is free of discrimination of all types from abusive, offensive or harassing behavior. Any employee who feels harassed or discriminated against should report the incident to his or her manager or to human resources.

VI. Quality assurance

We should maintain the quality of what we do through constant ongoing review with our clients, of all aims, activities, outcomes and the cost-effectiveness of every activity.
